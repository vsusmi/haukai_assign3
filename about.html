About Us

Haukai Restaurant specialises in Māori kai and is based in the Bay of Islands.

It was started by a couple who wanted to creat a business that would appeal to locals and tourists visiting the area.

We want customers to experience Te Ao Māori with the use of karakia for blessing food and other Māori culture experience
